Overview EN DBpedia (call sign: Tiny Diamond TD)


The DBpedia Tiny Diamond had many names before: DBpedia Core, EN DBpedia or some called it just DBpedia. In fact, it is a small, but very useful subset of the whole DBpedia extraction (EN generic/mappings + abstracts from other languages + community contributed links and ontologies). Tiny diamond is the refurbished equivalent of the "core" folder used for previous releases before 2020 (e.g. http://downloads.dbpedia.org/2016-10/core/). This subset is loaded to the DBpedia Main SPARQL endpoint. We are now providing this dataset as part of the community edition under the call sign “Tiny Diamond”, the smallest DBpedia kernel to glue Linked Open Data together.

Download (see tabs for bash, databus client, sparql and plain URL list): https://databus.dbpedia.org/dbpedia/collections/tiny-diamond-2021-06

DBpedia Global - Data Beyond Wikipedia

Archivo - a Unified Space to Access All Ontologies

DBpedia Archivo (archivo.dbpedia.org) is an online ontology interface and augmented archive. We are already providing a persistent backup for over 1354 ontologies (incl. versions) and might have become the most exhaustive unified space to discover and reach ontologies in the Semantic Web. What you can do to help: (1) add not-yet-discovered ontology, (2) check your own ontology and their rating and improve them to get ★★★★, (3) expert ontology users wanted to form a think tank on how to holistically evolve and validate the web of ontologies. Read more details and download the paper here.
